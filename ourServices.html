Admission

Our team is dedicated to ensuring that you find the best educational opportunities that match your academic goals and aspirations. With our support, you can confidently take the next step in your academic journey and achieve your dream of studying abroad. Let us help you unlock a world of possibilities!

Visa Services

At Nandan International, we provide comprehensive assistance for both student and visitor visa applications. Our team of experts guides you through the entire process, ensuring all documentation is complete and accurate. Whether you're applying for a study visa to pursue your academic goals abroad or a visitor visa for travel, we ensure a smooth and hassle-free experience. From filling out forms to preparing for interviews, we offer personalized support every step of the way. With our expertise, you can confidently navigate the visa application process and move one step closer to your international opportunities.

English Test Preparation

At Nandan International, we offer dedicated support for students preparing for English proficiency tests such as IELTS, TOEFL, Duolingo, PTE, and more. Our experienced instructors provide personalized coaching to help you improve your reading, writing, speaking, and listening skills. With tailored study plans, practice tests, and expert tips, we ensure you are fully prepared to achieve your target scores. Whether you’re aiming for university admission or visa approval, our training programs boost your confidence and language abilities. Let us guide you on your journey to success and help you achieve the best possible results on your English proficiency exams.

Statements of Purpose

At Nandan International, we specialize in creating impactful Statements of Purpose (SOP) for students seeking university admission and visa approval. Our expert team collaborates with each student to craft a personalized SOP that effectively communicates their academic journey, career aspirations, and reasons for choosing a particular course or university. We ensure that your SOP not only highlights your strengths and goals but also addresses key aspects required for visa approval. With our guidance, students can present a well-structured, compelling narrative that boosts their chances of securing admission and obtaining a student visa for their studies abroad.

All Guidance

Whether you're seeking admission to a top university or planning your next academic step, we support you throughout your entire journey.
